Why choose Renov-8?

Renov-8 are a specialist contemporary construction company who focus on unique, private domestic and commercial builds and renovations. From dream extensions to dream homes to dream retail outlets, we are a highly reputable outlet who collaborate with the best designers, trade-persons and architects across Ireland.

At Renov-8, we understand that every project is unique. Every build presents its own challenges that require unique solutions. We strive to assist our clients through the entire building process effortlessly, efficiently and happily.

We have over 30 years of experience and bring with us a wealth of knowledge, experience, a vast network of suppliers and most importantly a very high level of craftsmanship. We contract only the best trade-persons and the best quality materials so that your bespoke build stands the test of time.
